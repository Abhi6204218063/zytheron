# EVOTHERA
AI Oncology Simulation Platform

EVOTHERA is a computational oncology platform designed to simulate tumor evolution, therapy strategies, and resistance dynamics using mathematical modeling and AI-based optimization.

---

## Core Features

### Tumor Evolution Engine
Simulates tumor growth using evolutionary game theory and ecological models.

### Resistance Evolution Model
Models the emergence of drug-resistant cancer cell populations.

### Carrying Capacity Model
Introduces ecological limits to tumor growth dynamics.

### Tumor Heterogeneity Simulation
Simulates multiple tumor subclones competing over time.

---

## AI Therapy Engines

### Adaptive Therapy Controller
Dynamic therapy decisions based on tumor size.

### Reinforcement Learning Therapy Optimizer
AI agent learns optimal therapy strategies.

### Strategy Discovery Engine
Searches for optimal treatment schedules.

---

## Clinical Simulation

### Patient Digital Twin
Builds a computational twin of a cancer patient.

### Patient Parameter Inference
Estimates patient-specific tumor dynamics.

### Population Cancer Simulation
Simulates thousands of virtual patients.

### Virtual Clinical Trials
Evaluates therapy strategies across patient cohorts.
